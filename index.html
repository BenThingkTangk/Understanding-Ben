My output comes in violent waves, not balanced days.

Weekends

Why I sleep through weekends

After losing Coco, not having close friends around, and not having money or energy to really go do life, weekends became something else for me. If I am not working, there is too much room for grief, loneliness, and the feeling that life is standing still.

So weekends often become system-rest time. I sleep, shut down, avoid stimulation, and try to get enough strength back to survive another week. That does not mean I do not love you. It means I am trying not to break.
